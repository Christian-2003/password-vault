Privacy Policy for Password Vault

Last updated: 07/02/2024

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy informs you about how we collect, use and disclose your data when you use our mobile application Password Vault.

Information We Collect

We collect all personal and non-personal information that is directly entered into the app.

Furthermore, we collect information about all apps installed on your device at all times in order for you to be able to associate installed apps with provided account data.

Additionally, we can see all files within the shared storage of your device. However, these files cannot be opened without explicit approval of the user.

If you use the autofill service of Password Vault, we may collect all input data from other apps, including passwords and personal data, as well as information about the app in which they were entered.

How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data only for internal purposes in order to manage your account information and to provide autofill suggestions during login within other apps.

Data Storage

All data collected is stored locally on your device and is not transferred to external servers.

Collected data may be included in backups or exports. It is your responsibility to protect these backups and exports from third parties, since they may not be encrypted.

Data Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties unless required by law or necessary for the fulfillment of our services.

When you use the autofill service of Password Vault, we may share your data with other apps.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding your data or this Privacy Policy, please contact us through passwordvault@christian2003.de.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Please check this page regularly for updates.
